AIM AND SCOPE



Indicus Zoologica is one of the professional, peer-reviewed zoological and ornithological journal. The journal publishes original research articles, reviews, and short communications from all regions of the world. Manuscripts from all fields of, Zoology, Ornithology, Entomology, Pathology, Ecology, environment, Conservation are considered for publication, especially focusing on the ecology, morphology, behaviour, evolution, biogeography and conservation of animals. Studies advancing our understanding of living or extinct species, populations or assemblages are also accepted. With health benefits, and human welfare the papers from Microbiology, Biochemistry, Biotechnology and Ayurveda are also considered
